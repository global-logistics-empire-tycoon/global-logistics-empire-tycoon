How a delivery actually works

The simulation engine ticks every 5 in-game minutes and supports 1×–8× speed.

1️⃣

Accept

Pick a contract from the board (manually or via the Dispatcher). Feasibility is checked against your fleet.

2️⃣

Reposition

The assigned vehicle drives from its current location to the pickup city — consuming fuel along the way.

3️⃣

Load & transport

A fixed 0.5h loading phase, then the cargo is hauled along the routed network in real-time.

4️⃣

Deliver & earn

Payment, reputation (+0.4 base), wear, employee fatigue and offline catch-up are all settled.
